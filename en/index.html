About us

UCL Campus Malta, accredited in 2023 by the MFHEA under licence number 2023-015, belongs to Universidad Cum Laude – UCL (an entity registered in the Principality of Andorra with company registry number 22937 and NRT L-718011-S), whose corporate purpose is the promotion, implementation, management and operation of higher-education studies and institutions, as well as the development of training and university-level education projects. A university-level institution teaching both on site and online, offering degree and postgraduate studies, both official and institution-specific.

Through its commercial brand Universae Cum Laude it offers official Bachelor's, Master's and Doctoral degrees valid throughout the European Higher Education Area (EHEA), the United Kingdom and all Commonwealth countries.

Our mission

To close the gap between academic training and the reality of the labour market. We do not just provide higher education: we prepare professionals ready to add value from their first day in the company.

Official status and legal backing

We operate with full recognition under the MFHEA framework (the Maltese counterpart of Spain's ANECA): all our qualifications are official and fully valid within the EHEA — the assurance of regulated, internationally recognised education.

Degrees recognised in the UK and the Commonwealth

In addition to being official qualifications valid in the EHEA, they are also valid in the United Kingdom and in all Commonwealth member countries (Canada, Australia, New Zealand, etc.).

Three-year degrees

Our bachelor's degrees take three years (180 ECTS credits) and are equivalent to Spanish four-year, 240-ECTS degrees.

Our essence: practical, applied education

What sets us apart is an educational model designed from the workplace into the classroom.

  • Up-to-date content. Living, constantly evolving study plans that integrate the real needs companies demand at any given time.
  • Practical approach. We replace static theory with active methodologies where students learn to solve real problems.
  • Industry connection. Training based on projects, simulations and real cases that mirror the challenges professionals face every day.

The value of talent and employability

We understand that an official qualification is, above all, a key to professional success. That is why our institution focuses on:

  • Competence development. Beyond memorisation, we assess the ability to apply knowledge and contribute solutions.
  • Talent engine. A meeting point between young talent and company demand, both in the Maltese ecosystem and internationally.

Commitment to quality

At our institution, excellence is not a theoretical concept but the way we work. We guarantee a rigorous, transparent educational experience in which every program and every teacher is focused on strengthening our students' professional future and employability.
