Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Data controller

UCLCampus (trading name). Legal entity details, registered address, and tax ID pending completion by the project owner before going live. Contact: info@uclcampus.com.

What data we collect

Depending on how you interact with us, we may collect:

  • Identification data: full name, email, phone number.
  • Account data: email and password (always stored encrypted, never in plain text).
  • Academic and enrollment data: programs taken, enrollment status, grades where applicable.
  • Commercial data (if you contact us for information): source of contact, program of interest, and communication history, used internally in our CRM.
  • Minimal technical data: IP address and user agent in server security logs, and the session cookie while you are logged in.

What we use your data for

  • Managing your enrollment and giving you access to the learning platform.
  • Responding to your information requests.
  • Managing the commercial relationship with prospective students (internal CRM).
  • Complying with legal and contractual obligations arising from the educational service.
  • Sending you communications about our programs, only if you have given explicit consent (email marketing).

Legal basis

Performance of a contract (enrollment and delivery of the educational service), consent (marketing communications, non-essential cookies), and legitimate interest (service security, handling inquiries).

How long we keep your data

Student data is kept for the duration of the academic relationship and, after it ends, for the period required by applicable educational and tax regulations. Commercial contact data that does not convert to enrollment is kept for a maximum of 24 months from the last contact, unless you request earlier deletion.

Who we share your data with

We do not sell or share your data with third parties for purposes unrelated to our service. We may share data with technology providers strictly necessary to operate the platform (hosting, transactional email) under confidentiality and data processing agreements. Specifically, the contact form is sent via FormSubmit (formsubmit.co), a form-forwarding service that delivers your enquiry (first name, last name, email, country and message) to our mailbox and does not use it for any other purpose.

How we protect your data

Passwords are stored encrypted (bcrypt), communications travel encrypted (HTTPS/TLS), access to data is restricted by role, and we apply technical measures to protect against unauthorized access.

Your rights

You have the right to access, rectify, erase, object to, restrict the processing of, and request portability of your personal data. To exercise them, write to us at info@uclcampus.com.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the competent supervisory authority if you believe we have not handled your request correctly.

Minors

Our programs are aimed at adults. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy to reflect legal or service changes. We will always publish the last-updated date at the top of this page.

We use cookies

We use necessary cookies for the site to work and, only with your consent, analytics and marketing cookies. You can change your decision at any time from Cookie settings in the footer. More info in our Cookie Policy.
