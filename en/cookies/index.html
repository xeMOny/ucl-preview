What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that a website stores in your browser. They are used to remember information about your visit, such as your session or your preferences, and in some cases to analyze site usage or show relevant advertising.

Necessary cookies are always installed because the site cannot work without them (legitimate interest / performance of the service you request).
