Master of Business Administration (MBA)

This MBA program is designed for emerging and mid-career professionals who aspire to advance into leadership and strategic management roles across a range of sectors. It is particularly suited to young professionals seeking to accelerate their career towards managerial positions, mid-level managers looking to strengthen their strategic, financial and leadership capabilities, aspiring entrepreneurs wishing to develop advanced business acumen, and career changers from non-business fields who want to acquire comprehensive management knowledge and transition into business leadership roles. The added value of this MBA lies in its ability to bridge the gap between technical or functional expertise and advanced strategic leadership competencies.

Economics (Microeconomics and Macroeconomics)

10 ECTS

This module provides a comprehensive foundation in economic principles, covering both microeconomics and macroeconomics. Students will critically evaluate key economic concepts, including market structures, supply-demand dynamics and business cycles, to assess their impact on strategic business decisions.

Managerial Accounting / Business Accounting

10 ECTS

This module explores the fundamentals of managerial and business accounting. Students will learn to interpret financial statements, apply cost-accounting principles and use accounting information for strategic decision-making and organisational control.

Quantitative Methods in Business and Economics

10 ECTS

This module equips students with essential quantitative skills for business analysis. Topics include statistical methods, data-analysis techniques and mathematical modelling for business forecasting and evidence-based decision-making.

Research Methods

10 ECTS

This module develops students' research capabilities, covering research design, data-collection methodologies and analytical techniques. Students will acquire the skills needed to plan, conduct and present rigorous academic and professional research.

Globalisation and Business Strategy

10 ECTS

This module examines the impact of globalisation on business strategy. Students will analyse international business environments, cross-border competition and the development of strategies for sustainable competitive advantage in global markets.

Leadership and Organisational Behaviour

10 ECTS

This module explores leadership theories, organisational dynamics and behavioural science principles. Students will develop skills in people management, team leadership, driving organisational change and fostering high-performance work cultures.

Marketing Management (Digital Marketing, Social Media and E-Commerce)

10 ECTS

This module covers modern marketing management with an emphasis on digital transformation. Students will explore digital marketing strategies, social media marketing, e-commerce models and data-driven marketing techniques.

Financial Management

10 ECTS

This module provides a deep understanding of corporate finance and financial management. Topics include capital budgeting, financial planning, risk management, investment analysis and value-creation strategies.

Human Resources (HR)

10 ECTS

This module examines strategic human resources management, including talent acquisition, employee development, performance management, compensation strategies and HR policy development in contemporary organisations.

Master's Thesis

30 ECTS

The master's thesis is a mandatory element of the Master of Business Administration. Students will conduct independent research under faculty supervision, demonstrating advanced analytical skills and contributing original insights to business administration research.

  • • Qualification obtained. Qualifications issued in English, Spanish, French or Italian are accepted; for other languages, the qualification must be translated into English or Italian.
  • • Up-to-date CV in English.
  • • Copy of a valid identification document.

Language certifications (if you are not a native speaker and do not hold a qualification in these languages)

  • • English: TOEFL iBT, IELTS, Cambridge certificates, English Speaking Board (International) Ltd., Pearson English International Certificate, Trinity College London certificates, City & Guilds International ESOL (IESOL).
  • • Italian: CELI, CILS, AIL, PLIDA or IT certification.
  • • At least B2 level of English or Italian is required for bachelor's degrees; at least C1 level for master's degrees.

Legalisation of qualifications

  • • All educational qualifications (such as secondary school diploma, bachelor's, master's) must be apostilled by means of a certificate of value at the embassies of Malta or Italy abroad.
