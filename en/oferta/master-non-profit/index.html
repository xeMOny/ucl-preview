Master of Arts (MA) in Management and Leadership of Non-Profit Organisations

This master's degree aims to train leaders who are aware of the new challenges of the Third Sector and able to navigate the changing regulatory framework of social entities. These organisations play a crucial role in post-pandemic society, requiring both technical knowledge and visionary leadership.

Public Policy, Welfare and the Third Sector

6 ECTS

This module explores the crucial intersection between public policy, social welfare and the role played by the non-profit sector, particularly in response to the social welfare crisis.

Non-Profit Organisation Legislation

6 ECTS

This module examines the complexities of non-profit legislation, exploring the fundamental aspects of the legal framework governing the Third Sector.

Profile and Social Impact of the Third Sector, Networking and Organisational Dynamics

6 ECTS

This module explores crucial aspects of the Third Sector, delving into the management of local stakeholders and the bureaucratic and tax requirements essential for establishing a non-profit organisation.

Vision, Mission, Management and Control of a Non-Profit Organisation

6 ECTS

This module examines key aspects of the organisational dynamics of non-profit organisations, including volunteering and labour relations, fundraising methodologies, personnel-management challenges for both volunteers and employees, asset management, and the structures related to organisation, administration and accounting.

Human Resources Management and Development

12 ECTS

This module explores the complexities of human resources management and development in the context of the Third Sector.

Project Management

12 ECTS

This module explores the broad spectrum of project management in the context of the Third Sector.

Organisation of Association Events

6 ECTS

This module examines the complexities of organising association events within the framework of the Third Sector.

Focus on Human Rights

6 ECTS

This module explores the intersection between human rights and the work of the non-profit sector, and its role in public policy and social welfare.

Research Methods

6 ECTS

This module is designed for participants who need to acquire a critical understanding of research approaches and skills, together with the ability to apply research methods in their studies or professional life.

Master's Thesis

24 ECTS

The master's thesis is a mandatory element of the Master in Management and Leadership of Non-Profit Organisations.
